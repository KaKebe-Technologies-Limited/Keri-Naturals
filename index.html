About Us

Our Story

Keri Naturals began as a story of resilience and hope. Immaculate Akello, one of our founding members and a skilled product formulator, battled eczema from a young age. Conventional treatments offered little relief, but her discovery of the healing properties of shea butter changed everything. With consistent use, shea butter soothed her skin and inspired her to harness its potential to help others. This personal journey of healing coincided with a much larger issue in northern Uganda, where poverty and deforestation were devastating communities. Rural women, who are the custodians of shea trees, struggled to generate sustainable income. Additionally, the unchecked felling of shea trees for charcoal and firewood threatened this precious natural resource. These challenges inspired Keri Naturals to take action. We partnered with over 1,500 rural women to source shea nuts ethically, ensuring they earn fair wages while preserving this vital tree. By providing training and resources, we empower these women economically and promote sustainable practices. Beyond sourcing, we have committed to planting 20,000 shea trees in local communities to restore degraded landscapes and secure the future of this invaluable resource. Today, Keri Naturals represents more than skincare; it symbolizes hope, sustainability, and empowerment. From creating natural products that heal and nurture to restoring the environment and improving livelihoods, our journey is a testament to the power of community and nature. Together with our partners and customers, we’re building a future where the richness of northern Uganda’s heritage benefits generations to come.

Our Background

Keri Naturals is an innovative and eco-conscious beauty brand rooted in the heart of Africa. Founded with a mission to celebrate the continent’s rich natural resources, Keri Naturals focuses on the value addition of shea butter, a treasured ingredient renowned for its nourishing and healing properties. The brand collaborates closely with rural women, empowering them economically by sourcing raw materials directly from their communities. This approach not only ensures the highest quality products but also fosters sustainable livelihoods for these women.

At the core of Keri Naturals is a commitment to creating skincare solutions that blend traditional African wisdom with modern formulation techniques. The brand offers a range of products, from body butters to hair oils, catering to diverse skin and hair needs while maintaining a focus on natural, chemical-free formulations.

Keri Naturals also prioritizes cultural identity and African pride by incorporating African-inspired branding and names into its products. With a deep understanding of its customers’ needs and a passion for uplifting local communities, Keri Naturals is not just a beauty brand—it’s a movement that champions sustainability, inclusivity, and the rich heritage of Africa’s natural treasures.

Services

Empowering Beauty, Community, and Sustainability

At Keri Naturals, our services go beyond skincare—we’re dedicated to empowering communities, promoting sustainability, and providing expert guidance for your beauty and wellness journey. From personalized skin and hair consultations to eco-friendly initiatives, our services are designed to enhance your natural beauty while uplifting the world around us. Whether you’re seeking tailored advice to understand your skin type, need recommendations for the perfect products, or want to support a brand committed to environmental conservation and women’s empowerment, Keri Naturals is here for you. Premium Shea Butter Products

Keri Naturals offers a range of high-quality, natural skincare products made from sustainably sourced shea butter. These products include moisturizers, body oils, face creams, and hair care solutions designed to nourish and protect the skin and hair.

Skin Testing and Personalization

Keri Naturals provides a comprehensive skin type questionnaire to help customers determine their skin type and receive personalized product recommendations. This service ensures that each customer finds the most suitable products for their specific skincare needs.

Hair and Skin Consultancy

Keri Naturals offers a free 15-minute consultation and paid 1-hour sessions for personalized skin and hair advice. The consultations focus on addressing specific skin or hair concerns, recommending appropriate products, and providing tips for better care.

Sustainability and Empowerment Initiatives

Keri Naturals is dedicated to empowering women in rural communities by providing training, fair wages, and access to the shea butter market. Through this initiative, the company fosters economic growth and supports women’s independence.

Eco-Friendly Practices

Keri Naturals is committed to sustainability, ensuring that all practices, from sourcing shea butter to packaging products, are environmentally friendly. The company actively participates in tree planting initiatives and promotes eco-conscious methods throughout its operations.

Donation and Community Suppor

Keri Naturals offers an opportunity for customers and supporters to donate to the cause. Funds raised help support women empowerment programs, community development, and environmental sustainability initiatives.
