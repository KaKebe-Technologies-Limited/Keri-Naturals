Mission Statement

Sustainability and eco-friendliness are at the heart of everything we do.

About Us

Our Story

The story of Keri Naturals begins with a vision to combine the power of nature with the drive to empower women. Founded by Ms. Immaculate, the company’s journey started with a deep recognition of the challenges faced by rural women involved in the collection and production of shea butter. Witnessing their struggles inspired her to create a brand that not only offered high-quality, natural skincare but also helped uplift these women and their communities.
Initially, Keri Naturals operated on a small scale, focusing on sourcing raw, unrefined shea butter from women in rural Africa. Through fair trade practices and providing better access to markets, the company sought to ensure that these women were paid fairly for their labor and empowered to achieve economic independence. With a focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing, Keri Naturals also began to implement eco-friendly practices and support reforestation efforts to protect the environment and preserve the shea tree populations vital to their supply chain.

The name "Keri" symbolizes "care," reflecting the company’s commitment to caring for both its customers and the communities it serves. This care goes beyond the products it creates, encompassing a broader mission of empowerment and environmental sustainability. What started as a humble initiative has now evolved into a thriving brand with a global mission to revolutionize the skincare industry. Keri Naturals remains rooted in its core values—empowering women, protecting the environment, and providing quality, natural beauty products—making a lasting impact on both people and the planet.

Our Background

Keri Naturals was born out of a passion for natural beauty and a deep commitment to social impact. The company’s founder, Ms. Immaculate, recognized a unique opportunity to combine the rich, age-old tradition of shea butter production with the empowerment of women in rural Africa.
After witnessing firsthand the challenges faced by rural women, particularly those involved in the shea nut collection process, she felt compelled to create a business that could help uplift these communities while delivering high-quality skincare products to the world.

In the beginning, Keri Naturals was a small-scale venture that focused on sourcing pure, unrefined shea butter from rural women farmers, ensuring that they received fair wages for their hard work. The company worked closely with these women to establish fair trade practices and support their growth by providing them with better access to markets and sustainable livelihoods.

The name "Keri" comes from the word "care" which reflects the company’s ethos—caring for both its customers and the communities it serves. Through this model, Keri Naturals began to grow, not just as a skincare brand, but as a beacon of empowerment for women and a champion of environmental sustainability. In addition to its mission of creating high-quality skincare products, Keri Naturals is deeply committed to environmental responsibility. The company works to protect the environment by using eco-friendly packaging and supporting reforestation projects to replenish the shea trees that are critical to the supply chain. What began as a small initiative has now blossomed into a thriving company with global ambitions.

Services

Empowering Beauty, Community, and Sustainability

At Keri Naturals, our services go beyond skincare—we’re dedicated to empowering communities, promoting sustainability, and providing expert guidance for your beauty and wellness journey. From personalized skin and hair consultations to eco-friendly initiatives, our services are designed to enhance your natural beauty while uplifting the world around us. Whether you’re seeking tailored advice to understand your skin type, need recommendations for the perfect products, or want to support a brand committed to environmental conservation and women’s empowerment, Keri Naturals is here for you. Let us help you embrace your natural glow while making a positive impact!

Premium Shea Butter Products

Keri Naturals offers a range of high-quality, natural skincare products made from sustainably sourced shea butter. These products include moisturizers, body oils, face creams, and hair care solutions designed to nourish and protect the skin and hair.

Skin Testing and Personalization

Keri Naturals provides a comprehensive skin type questionnaire to help customers determine their skin type and receive personalized product recommendations. This service ensures that each customer finds the most suitable products for their specific skincare needs.

Hair and Skin Consultancy

Keri Naturals offers a free 15-minute consultation and paid 1-hour sessions for personalized skin and hair advice. The consultations focus on addressing specific skin or hair concerns, recommending appropriate products, and providing tips for better care.

Sustainability and Empowerment Initiatives

Keri Naturals is dedicated to empowering women in rural communities by providing training, fair wages, and access to the shea butter market. Through this initiative, the company fosters economic growth and supports women’s independence.

Eco-Friendly Practices

Keri Naturals is committed to sustainability, ensuring that all practices, from sourcing shea butter to packaging products, are environmentally friendly. The company actively participates in tree planting initiatives and promotes eco-conscious methods throughout its operations.

Donation and Community Suppor

Keri Naturals offers an opportunity for customers and supporters to donate to the cause. Funds raised help support women empowerment programs, community development, and environmental sustainability initiatives.
